BLUE LUNGS
Detecting Dissolved Oxygen levels in Aquatic Environments

About

Our project

The "Blue Lungs" initiative could involve developing an oxygen detection device that can be deployed in a varietyof bodies of water, including oceans, lakes, and rivers.The system could include multiple sensors that measure dissolved oxygen levels at various depths and locations,giving a comprehensive picture of the oxygen levels in the water.The data gathered by the sensors could be wirelessly transmitted to a centralized hub or cloud based platform to make the system more user friendly and accessible.This could enable real time tracking of oxygen levels, pH level, temperature, and alerting when levels fall below a certain threshold.
